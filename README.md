# Netflix-Analytics-Dashboard-
Developed an interactive Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard using Excel, Pivot Tables, and data visualization techniques to analyze Netflix's global content library. The project transforms raw streaming data into actionable insights that help understand content distribution, audience targeting, geographic reach, and content trends over time.  

# Business Problems Solved
## 1. Content Mix Analysis

Problem: Netflix needs to understand whether its library is dominated by Movies or TV Shows.

Solution: Created visualizations showing the distribution of Movies versus TV Shows, helping stakeholders understand content allocation and programming strategy.

## 2. Content Growth Trend Analysis

Problem: Decision-makers need visibility into how Netflix's content catalog has grown over time.

Solution: Analyzed content releases by year to identify growth patterns, peak production periods, and shifts in content strategy.

## 3. Geographic Content Distribution

Problem: Netflix requires insights into which countries contribute the most content to its platform.

Solution: Built country-level analysis highlighting major content-producing regions and identifying opportunities for regional expansion.

## 4. Audience Targeting Insights

Problem: Understanding which age groups and audience segments receive the most content investment.

Solution: Analyzed content ratings (TV-MA, TV-14, PG-13, etc.) to reveal audience targeting strategies and content maturity distribution.

## 5. Genre Performance Analysis

Problem: Content teams need to identify the most common genres and categories available on the platform.

Solution: Created genre-based reporting to determine which content categories dominate the Netflix catalog.

## 6. Content Acquisition Strategy

Problem: Management needs visibility into when content was added to the platform.

Solution: Analyzed "Date Added" trends to understand acquisition patterns and content expansion strategies.

# Key Insights
- Movies account for the majority of Netflix content (over 6,100 titles).
- TV Shows represent approximately 2,600 titles.
- The United States is the largest content contributor, followed by India and the United Kingdom.
- TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common audience ratings, indicating a strong focus on mature and young adult viewers.
- Content production accelerated significantly in recent years before 2021.
